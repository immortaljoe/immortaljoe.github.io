We have finalized the app map of our project. The top left part represents hardware settings and right part indicates details for software design. As shown in the diagram, we utilized the tree structure to describe functionalities of each part.

For the hardware part, customer should buy our products firstly and set the wireless charge base in the inner room. After that, the application must be installed on a mobile devices in order to manage the drones and maps of properties.
